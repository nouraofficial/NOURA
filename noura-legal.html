📜 Privacy Policy
Your Trust is More Valuable Than Your Data
We believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a premium feature. Every person who uses Noura deserves to understand exactly what information is collected, why it is needed, and how it is protected.

Our Promise

We are committed to communicating our practices in clear language rather than confusing legal jargon. Your data belongs to you.

👤
Account Information
Full name, username, email address, phone number (optional), and profile picture when you create an account.
🍽️
Food Preferences
Favourite cuisines, dietary preferences, allergies, nutrition goals, meal preferences, and saved recipes to personalise your experience.
📍
Location
Only when you grant permission. Used to find nearby restaurants, improve recommendations, and estimate distances. Disable at any time.
📱
Device Information
Device model, operating system, app version, and crash reports to improve app performance and stability.

🌍 The Noura Foundation
Building More Than an App
Noura is a commitment to making food discovery, meal planning, and local food businesses more accessible, trustworthy, and intelligent through technology.
"Does this genuinely help people?"
Every decision we make begins with this question. Technology should never replace humanity. It should empower it.
The Noura Charter
Food connects people. Across cultures. Across generations. Across communities.

Noura exists to celebrate that connection. We believe technology should never distance people from food — it should bring them closer to it. Closer to local businesses. Closer to healthier choices. Closer to family traditions. Closer to new experiences.

Whether you are a customer discovering your next meal, a restaurant serving your neighbourhood, a creator sharing recipes, or a small business chasing a dream — you belong here.
⭐ Core Values
What We Stand For
These values are not aspirational posters on a wall. They are the criteria against which we measure every product decision.
1
Trust First
Trust is earned through honesty, transparency, and consistency. We never take it for granted.
2
People Before Profit
Long-term success comes from improving lives. Revenue follows value. Never the other way around.
3
Food For Everyone
Every culture. Every cuisine. Every tradition. Every table. Everyone belongs.
4
Innovation With Purpose
Technology should simplify life, not complicate it. Every feature must solve a real problem.
5
Privacy By Design
Privacy is not an afterthought. It is part of the product from day one.
6
Quality Over Quantity
One outstanding experience is worth more than a hundred forgettable ones.
7
Grow Together
When customers succeed, restaurants grow, creators thrive, and communities flourish.
