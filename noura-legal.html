Privacy Policy

Noura ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what choices you have.

What we collect

  • Account info: name, username, email, and password (handled securely by our authentication provider — we never see or store your raw password).
  • Profile details you choose to add: bio, country, cuisine preferences, dietary restrictions, allergies.
  • Content you create: reviews, ratings, saved favourites, uploaded photos, challenge participation.
  • Vendor information: business details, menu items, and contact info, if you register as a vendor.
  • Usage data: basic app interactions (e.g. which screens you visit) to keep the app working correctly.

How we use it

  • To run your account and show you your saved content across devices.
  • To display vendor listings, menus, and reviews to other users.
  • To power Noura AI's recommendations — your questions to the AI are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a response.
  • To send essential account emails (password resets, confirmations) and, if you opt in, announcements about new features or challenges.

Who we share it with

We don't sell your data. We use a small number of service providers to run Noura, each of whom only receives what they need to do their job:

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Google (Gemini API) — processes your messages to Noura AI to generate responses.
  • TheMealDB / Edamam — public recipe data sources; we send them search terms, not your personal info.
  • Resend — delivers transactional and announcement emails.

Your rights

You can update or delete most of your profile information directly in the app (Profile → Edit). To request a full export or deletion of your account data, email us at nouraofficial.app@gmail.com and we'll act on it within a reasonable time.

Children

Noura isn't intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under that age.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

What Noura is

Noura is a food discovery platform — it helps you find restaurants, vendors, and recipes, and helps food businesses build an online presence. Noura does not currently handle food delivery, in-app ordering, or payments. Any transaction (ordering, payment, delivery) happens directly between you and the vendor, outside of Noura.

Your account

  • You're responsible for keeping your login credentials secure.
  • You must provide accurate information when creating an account or a vendor listing.
  • You're responsible for content you post — reviews, photos, and business listings should be honest and your own.

Acceptable use

Don't use Noura to: post fake reviews, harass other users or vendors, upload content you don't have rights to, impersonate a business you don't own, or attempt to abuse or overload the platform (including automated/bot access).

Vendor listings

Vendors are responsible for the accuracy of their own menu, pricing, hours, and contact information. Noura does not verify every claim made by a vendor and is not responsible for the quality, safety, or accuracy of food or services provided by listed businesses.

AI-generated content

Noura AI's recipe suggestions, nutrition notes, and recommendations are generated by an AI model and are informational only — they are not medical, dietary, or professional advice. Always check ingredients against your own allergies and consult a professional for medical or nutrition guidance.

Limitation of liability

Our Mission

Noura exists to help people find great local food, and to help the businesses behind that food grow — without needing to hire a web developer or pay for advertising they can't afford. A local food seller with no website should be able to sign up and, in minutes, have a professional page, a real menu, and a way for people to find and reach them.

We're building this in public, one honest feature at a time.

Core Values

  • Honesty over hype. No fake reviews, no fake data, no inflated numbers — what you see on Noura is real.
  • Small businesses first. Every feature we build should make it easier for a local vendor to be found and to grow.
  • Accessible by default. Free to use for consumers, free basic listings for vendors — premium features come later, without locking out anyone who needs the basics.
  • Built for Africa, useful anywhere. Starting with Nigerian food culture and local realities, designed to scale further.
