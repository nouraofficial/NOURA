Privacy Policy
Our Promise
Your trust is more valuable than your data. We believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a premium feature.
Information We Collect
Depending on how you use Noura, we may collect:
Account Information
Full name, username, email address
Phone number (optional)
Profile picture
Food Preferences
Favourite cuisines, dietary preferences, allergies
Nutrition goals and meal preferences
Saved recipes
Activity
Restaurants viewed, recipes created
Search history, favourite vendors
Location
Only when permission has been granted. Used to find nearby restaurants, improve recommendations, and estimate distances. You may disable this at any time.
Why We Collect Information
Personalise meal recommendations
Improve AI suggestions
Save your preferences
Connect you with local food businesses
Improve app performance
Prevent fraud and abuse
Respond to customer support requests
Data Protection
Your information is protected using modern security practices. We continuously work to encrypt sensitive information, protect user accounts, detect suspicious activity, and prevent unauthorised access. Although no online system is completely immune to risk, protecting user data remains one of our highest priorities.
Sharing Information
We never sell your personal information. Information may only be shared when necessary — to help restaurants fulfil your requests, work with trusted service providers, comply with applicable laws, or protect users from abuse.
AI Transparency
AI powers many experiences inside Noura — meal suggestions, recipe generation, restaurant recommendations. These suggestions are informational only. They are not medical, dietary, or professional advice. For medical conditions or specialised nutrition needs, please consult qualified professionals.
Your Rights
Access your personal information
Correct inaccurate information
Delete your account
Request deletion of personal data
Control notification preferences
Withdraw location permission at any time
Questions about your privacy?
Contact us at privacy@noura.app
Terms of Service
By using Noura, you agree to these terms. Please read them carefully.
Using Noura
Noura is a food discovery and meal planning platform. You must be at least 13 years old to create an account. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure.
Community Code of Conduct
Noura is built around respect. By joining, you agree to help create a welcoming environment for everyone.
You must not:
Harass, intimidate, or discriminate against others
Post fake reviews, false claims, or misleading information
Share hate speech, violent, or abusive content
Send spam, scams, or phishing content
Publish another person's private information without consent
Vendor Standards
Food businesses on Noura agree to maintain food safety standards, display accurate menus with clear pricing, identify allergens where possible, and communicate professionally with customers. Misleading images, false claims, or impersonation of other businesses is strictly prohibited.
AI-Generated Content
Noura clearly identifies AI-generated content. AI meal plans, recipes, and suggestions are informational only — not medical or dietary advice. We will never manipulate users into spending money through AI.
Intellectual Property
Content you create on Noura belongs to you. By posting, you grant Noura a licence to display it within the platform. You may not use Noura's brand, logo, or technology without permission.
Enforcement
Violations of these terms may result in content removal, warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent account removal. Serious violations may be reported to relevant authorities.
Changes to These Terms
We may update these terms as Noura grows. We will notify you of significant changes. Continued use of Noura after changes means you accept the updated terms.
Questions about these terms?
Contact us at legal@noura.app

📖 The Noura Constitution
We believe:
We will never intentionally manipulate users into spending more money.
We will clearly identify AI-generated content.
We will never sell users' personal information.
We will prioritise accessibility so that as many people as possible can use our platform.
We will design with empathy before efficiency.
We will support local businesses alongside larger brands.
We will build responsibly, even when it is harder.
We will admit mistakes, learn from them, and improve continuously.
We will measure success not only by revenue, but by the value we create for people.
We will protect the trust placed in us every single day.
